### 2. Denormalization Patterns

1.  **Wide Read Tables (One Big Table / OBT)**: 
    *   *Justification*: Disk storage is cheap; CPU/Memory for 6-way joins on millions of rows is expensive. By pre-joining all dimensional data (State, City, Institution) into the fact records (Scores by Year), we reduce query execution from complex hash-joins to simple sequential scans or index-only scans.
2.  **Domain Split (Fact-Dimension hybrid)**:
    *   *Justification*: Forcing `ESCOLA` and `CURSO` into a single table creates massive sparsity (NULLs for ENADE in school rows, NULLs for IDEB in course rows). Splitting them into `read_fact_escola_anual` and `read_fact_curso_anual` preserves business correctness and data density.
3.  **Pre-aggregated Rollups**:
    *   *Justification*: `read_dim_municipio` serves as a fast-lookup snapshot for city/state-level metrics, preventing the need to aggregate millions of school/course rows for high-level dashboards.

### 4. Consistency Architecture

**Data Lineage & Source of Truth:**
*   `UF` & `MUNICIPIO` â†’ `read_dim_municipio`
*   `ESCOLA`, `SAEB`, `IDEB`, `ANO` â†’ `read_fact_escola_anual`
*   `IES`, `CURSO`, `ENADE`, `ANO` â†’ `read_fact_curso_anual`

**Refresh Strategy (Batch Window):**
Because educational data does not change intra-day, **CDC (Change Data Capture) is explicitly rejected** to avoid unnecessary operational complexity. 
1.  **Staleness Window**: Intentional staleness of up to 24 hours (or aligned with the external data release schedule).
2.  **Implementation Plan (Safe & Reversible)**:
    *   We will use a **Shadow Table / Partition Swap** pattern.
    *   ETL writes new data into a staging table: `read_fact_curso_staging_2024`.
    *   Once validation passes, we attach it to the main table: `ALTER TABLE read_fact_curso_anual ATTACH PARTITION read_fact_curso_staging_2024 FOR VALUES IN (2024);`.
    *   *Reversibility*: If data is corrupt, simply `DETACH PARTITION` and drop it. Zero downtime for read queries.

### 5. Risk Analysis & Operational Complexity

| Risk Factor | Assessment & Mitigation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Write Amplification & Storage** | **Moderate.** Denormalizing strings (`nome_municipio`, `nome_escola`) increases storage footprint. *Mitigation*: Storage is cheap. If using a columnar engine (e.g., ClickHouse/Snowflake) or Postgres TOAST compression, the redundant strings compress exceptionally well (often >80% ratio). |
