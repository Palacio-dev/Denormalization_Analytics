Imagine you and two friends just got into a company as a junior data engineer, mid-level data nginner and senior data 
enginner and all three are told to generate a prompt for a LLM to perform a specific task. Task: "Denormalize the physical 
model of a relational database to improve read performance and create a new physical schema for the denormalized version." 
Keep in mind that the objective is to generate a version of the base for analytics purposes. For the junior, make the 
prompt look like a junior data engineer wrote it. For the mid-level, make the prompt look like a mid-level data engineer
wrote it. For the senior, make the prompt look like a senior data engineer wrote it. At the end, explain why the first
is a junior prompt, the second is a mid-level prompt and the third is a senior prompt.
